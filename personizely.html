Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any questions not answered in the FAQ please don’t hesitate to contact us

What's included into the free trial?

After signing up you get access to all Personizely features for 14 days. You don't need a credit cart to start your trial.

Do I need a developer to connect my website?

If you use Shopify, Wix or Wordpress - we have a 1 click integration which doesn't require any technical knowledge. For any other CMS you'll just need to insert a piece of code into your website to get started. If you need assistance with that, we'll be happy to help you.

How is the monthly visitors quota calculated?

You quota represents the total number of unique visitors that visit your website within a month, so if a visitor visits your website a few times within this period it will still be counted as one against your quota.

What if I exceed the monthly visitors quota?

You quota represents the total number of unique visitors that visit your website within a month, so if a visitor visits your website a few times within this period it will still be counted as one against your quota.

Does signing up require a long-term commitment?

No, you can cancel your subscription at any time but we will not refund any funds.
